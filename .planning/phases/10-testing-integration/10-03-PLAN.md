**2. Distance-Based Profile Distribution (2 tests):**

- `close sectors are predominantly balanced profiles (200+ samples)` — Generate 300 sectors at distance < 5 from origin (random angles, radius 0-4.5) via sectorManager.initializeSector(x, y, true). **3. Rarity Distribution (1-2 tests):**

- `discovery bonus exclusive signals are ~80% epic, ~20% legendary over 100+ samples` — Run spawnDiscoveryBonusSignals 150 times for a Resource-Rich sector. Zero common/uncommon/rare exclusive signals.

- `standard signal rarity distribution has common as most frequent` — Generate 500 signal rarities via probeManager.determineSignalRarity(false, null). Verify common is the most frequent rarity (> 40% of samples). Verify legendary is the least frequent (< 10%). This confirms the base rarity curve is healthy.
