# Probethium Economy Design

## Core Philosophy
Probethium should feel **precious** and **meaningful**. Every Probethium spent should feel like an important decision, not just another resource grind. It's the "endgame currency" that unlocks transformative upgrades and prestigious cosmetics.

---

## 💎 Tier 1: Game-Changing Upgrades (High Value)

### 1. **Quantum Entanglement** (500 Probethium)
- Link two hubs to share resources instantly
- Eliminates shuttle logistics between distant sectors
- Strategic choice: which hubs to link?
- **Why it's valuable**: Transforms late-game expansion strategy

### 2. **Probe Automation AI** (300 Probethium)
- Probes automatically redeploy after returning
- Auto-selects optimal exploration routes based on signal density
- Reduces micromanagement in late game
- **Why it's valuable**: Quality of life + efficiency boost

### 3. **Signal Amplifier** (250 Probethium per hub)
- Increases signal spawn rate in hub's sector by 50%
- Stacks with research bonuses
- Permanent upgrade per hub
- **Why it's valuable**: Accelerates resource collection

### 4. **Exotic Matter Synthesizer** (400 Probethium)
- Unlocks new resource type: Exotic Matter
- Required for Tier 3 technologies
- Opens entirely new research branch
- **Why it's valuable**: Gate to endgame content

### 5. **Time Dilation Field** (600 Probethium)
- Increases probe speed by 100% in equipped hub's sector
- Affects all probes from that hub
- Per-hub upgrade
- **Why it's valuable**: Doubles exploration efficiency

### 6. **Mining Station Catalyst** (350 Probethium)
- Doubles Probethium production rate
- Applies to ALL mining stations
- One-time global upgrade
- **Why it's valuable**: Exponential Probethium growth

### 7. **Sector Mapper** (200 Probethium)
- Reveals all signals in current sector without scanning
- Permanent upgrade
- Shows signal rarity before collection
- **Why it's valuable**: Perfect information = optimal strategy

## ⚙️ Tier 3: Meta Progression (Very High Value)

### 1. **Prestige System** (1000 Probethium)
- Reset all progress
- Gain "Cosmic Echoes" based on Probethium earned
- Echoes provide permanent bonuses across all runs:
  - +1% resource gain per 10 Echoes
  - +1% probe speed per 10 Echoes
  - +1% signal spawn per 10 Echoes
- **Why it's valuable**: Infinite replayability

### 2. **Research Point Boost** (500 Probethium)
- Gain +50% research points from all sources
- Permanent account-wide boost
- Stackable up to 3 times
- **Why it's valuable**: Accelerates progression

### 3. **Blueprint Library** (750 Probethium)
- Saves 3 hub layouts as "blueprints"
- Quick-deploy entire probe networks
- Includes probe routes and building placements
- **Why it's valuable**: Efficiency for advanced players

---

## 🎯 Tier 4: Unique Mechanics (Ultra Rare)

### 1. **Wormhole Generator** (2000 Probethium)
- Create permanent portals between two points
- Probes can instantly travel through wormholes
- Max 3 wormholes
- **Why it's valuable**: Completely changes map traversal

### 2. **Dyson Sphere Fragment** (5000 Probethium)
- Passive Probethium generation (1 per hour)
- Purely idle income
- Ultimate endgame achievement
- **Why it's valuable**: Prestige symbol + idle gains

### 3. **Universal Translator** (3000 Probethium)
- Unlocks "First Contact" events
- Random encounters with alien probes
- Trade resources, gain unique technologies
- New narrative elements
- **Why it's valuable**: Adds story and mystery

## 💰 Probethium Earning Rate Balance

**Early Game** (0-2 hours):
- First mining station: ~0.001 Probethium/min
- With resources: ~5 Probethium/hour
- Goal: Save for first 50P cosmetic or 200P upgrade

**Mid Game** (2-10 hours):
- Multiple stations + research: ~0.005 Probethium/min
- With optimal setup: ~30 Probethium/hour
- Goal: Major upgrades (300-500P)

**Late Game** (10+ hours):
- Optimized network: ~0.02 Probethium/min
- With catalyst: ~0.04 Probethium/min
- ~150 Probethium/hour
- Goal: Prestige, wormholes, Dyson sphere

## 🎮 Psychological Design

**Make Probethium Feel Special:**
1. **Visual feedback**: Glowing purple particle effects when earned
2. **Sound design**: Special "ding" sound when Probethium collected
3. **Rarity**: Keep earn rate low enough that each point matters
4. **Choice**: Upgrades should be meaningful tradeoffs
5. **Exclusivity**: Some cosmetics should be hard to get (bragging rights)

**Avoid These Traps:**
- ❌ Don't make Probethium required for basic progression
- ❌ Don't make cosmetics feel mandatory
- ❌ Don't make earn rate too fast (devalues currency)
- ❌ Don't gate content behind absurd grind (5000P should be months, not years)

## 🎯 Design Goal Summary

**Probethium should be:**
- ✅ Valuable but not grindy
- ✅ Transformative when spent on upgrades
- ✅ Expressive when spent on cosmetics
- ✅ Strategic (meaningful choices)
- ✅ Rewarding (tangible benefits)
- ✅ Prestigious (show off your progress)

**The player should think:**
> "Should I save for the 500P Quantum Entanglement that will change my strategy, or spend 150P on that awesome void trail effect that looks incredible?"

That tension = good design! 🎮
